Rules

  1. You will be provided with 220V 50 HZ AC power.
  2. You can’t supply more than 120W power to your robot.
  3. Voltage must not be more than 24V between any two points.
  4. If robot is supplied with AC power, RMS voltage between any two points can’t be greater than 24V.

  9. Radio jammers are not allowed.
  10. Robots must not weigh more than 5Kg.
